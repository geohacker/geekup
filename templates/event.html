Divya Manian

Divya Manian is a web opener for Opera Software in Seattle. She made the jump from developing device drivers for Motorola phones to designing websites and has not looked back since. She takes her duties as an Open Web vigilante seriously which has resulted in collaborative projects such as HTML5 Boilerplate.

HTML5 Boilerplate

In this talk, you will learn how you can use HTML5 Boilerplate to get your projects up and running quickly while keeping best practices covered.

Divya will start with an overview of HTML5 Boilerplate before diving into some examples and demos to give you a feel for how you can jumpstart your next project with HTML5 Boilerplate.

Bangalore's own web geeks will open the evening with presentations on their work.
